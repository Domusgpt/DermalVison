# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-23

## 1. Introduction
DermalVision ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Data
We may collect personally identifiable information, such as:
- Name and email address (via Firebase Auth).
- Profile picture.
- Skin type (Fitzpatrick scale).

### b. Sensitive Health Data
- **Skin Images:** Photos of your skin captured via the App.
- **Analysis Results:** AI-generated assessments of skin conditions.
- **Chat Logs:** Conversations with the SkinShurpa AI assistant.

### c. Usage Data
- Device information (model, OS version).
- Log data (IP address, access times).

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the App.
- To improve our AI models (with your explicit consent).
- To send you notifications about your skin health.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Security
We use industry-standard security measures, including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
- Strict access controls via Firebase Security Rules.
- PII redaction in logs.

## 5. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your data.
- Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
- Export your data in a portable format.
